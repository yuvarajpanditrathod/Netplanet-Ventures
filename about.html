Our Story

A showroom-first company that grew by keeping promises.

Netplanet Ventures began as a boutique installer focused on mosquito screens that actually fit Indian homes. Today, we partner with architects, hoteliers, and homeowners across Karnataka—still obsessing over measurements, hardware, and finishes so every frame sits clean and works smoothly.

2018 — Foundation

Set up our first fabrication pod and on-site survey crew.

2018 — Showroom

Launched experience showroom so clients could see, feel, and compare systems.

2018 — Scale

Expanded product stack with pleated sliders, roller blinds, and custom hardware.

Now — Forward

Pursuing faster lead times, greener materials, and data-backed quality checks.

Precision fabrication

Sustainable mindset

Process

A proven, showroom-led delivery playbook.

Every build follows a documented sequence so you know where we are, what is next, and when your space will be ready.

01

Showroom consult

Compare meshes, profiles, and fabrics in-person with our specialists.

02

On-site survey

Laser measurements, obstruction checks, and hardware mapping.

03

Fabrication

CNC-cut frames, tension-balanced mesh, and pre-hung checks before dispatch.

04

Installation & care

Clean installs, handover walkthroughs, warranty registration, and scheduled service reminders.
