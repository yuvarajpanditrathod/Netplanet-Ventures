Expert Solutions

Frequently Asked Questions

Discover answers about mosquito nets for windows, openable frames, and our range of home improvement solutions including ceiling dryers and aluminium fixtures.

How does an openable mosquito net work?
Unlike sliding screens, an openable mosquito net swings open like a small door on a hinge. This allows you to easily clean the glass or remove your aluminium window grille without uninstalling the mesh.
Can it fit on aluminium windows?
Yes! Our frames are powder-coated to match existing aluminium windows, alu windows, and domal window profiles. We ensure the netting window sits flush for a seamless look.
Do you install laundry hanger ceiling systems?
Absolutely. Alongside mosquito net on the window installations, we offer ceiling mounted clothes drying rack and ceiling dryer pulley systems. If you need a cloth drying hanger with pulley, we have space-saving solutions.
Do you install on aluminium door bathroom fixtures?
We provide custom solutions for aluminium door bathroom and kitchen aluminium door needs. Our frames blend perfectly with aluminium window grilles and standard aluminium window frames for a unified look.
Is it easy to clean a mosquito net for the window?
Yes, this is the main advantage. The frame swings open completely, allowing you to wipe the glass and maintain your aluminium window frames without obstruction. It is the best mosquito net for the window for maintenance.
Is this better than sliding mosquito net?
It depends on usage. Openable frames are better for cleaning glass and grills, while sliding mosquito net for windows is better for very wide spans or balcony doors where you need partial opening without fully swinging the frame.
