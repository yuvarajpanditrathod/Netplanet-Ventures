Frequently Asked Questions

Discover answers about mosquito nets for doors, hinged screens, and our range of home improvement solutions including ceiling dryers and aluminium fixtures.

Is a hinged mosquito net better for main doors?
Yes, for main entrances, a hinged system offers more durability and security than magnetic mosquito net or velcro mosquito net for windows. It functions like a traditional door, making it ideal for high-traffic areas requiring a robust mosquito net for doors.
Can you install on aluminium doors?
Absolutely. Our frames are designed to match aluminium windows and prices to fit your budget. We ensure a perfect fit on aluminium door with window combos, alu windows, and standard aluminium sliding window frames.
Do you install laundry hanger ceiling systems?
Yes, alongside mosquito mesh installation, we offer ceiling mounted clothes drying rack and ceiling dryer pulley systems. If you are looking for a cloth drying hanger with pulley, pulley clothes drying rack, or steel ceiling cloth dryer stand, we have solutions to save space.
Can you install on aluminium door bathroom fixtures?
We specialize in fitting mosquito net for doors near me for specific fixtures like aluminium door bathroom units and window door aluminium setups. We provide color-matched frames that blend perfectly with your existing aluminium window frames.
Is it suitable for balcony mosquito net?
Yes, hinged doors are excellent for balcony access where mosquito netting for balcony is needed. They provide a wide opening for entry, distinct from sliding doors with mosquito net which might restrict partial opening.
Are your solutions pet-friendly and durable?
Yes, our heavy-duty window screens and pet-friendly screens for homes are designed to withstand wear. We use strong mesh and durable hinges for mosquito nets for sliding doors and hinged systems alike.
