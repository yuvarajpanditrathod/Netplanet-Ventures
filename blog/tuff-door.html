Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about our Tuff Door, stainless steel mesh, and how we match your interior designs with premium color finishes.

Is Tuff Door really pet-proof?
Yes. Unlike standard fiberglass or polyester meshes, our pet proof mosquito net uses hand-woven stainless steel (SS304 or SS316). It is virtually impossible for dogs or cats to tear or scratch through, making it ideal for heavy traffic mosquito nets.
Can you match my interior colors?
Absolutely. We offer specialized powder coating that can mimic wood grain textures or match any RAL color. Whether you have aluminium window frames, wooden doors, or UPVC, we ensure the Tuff Door blends seamlessly with your home's decor.
What is the difference between Tuff Door and Ace Door?
While Ace Door is designed for elegance and standard residential use, the Tuff Door is an industrial-grade solution. It features thicker profiles, heavier stainless steel mosquito mesh, and reinforced hinges, making it suitable for security purposes and rough handling.
Is the stainless steel mesh see-through?
Yes. The mesh is woven with high-tensile micro wires that provide excellent visibility and airflow while maintaining security. It allows you to enjoy your view without the visual obstruction of bars or grilles.
Does it require special maintenance?
Very little. Due to the high-quality aluminium window frames and stainless steel construction, it is rust-resistant. Simply wiping it down with a damp cloth periodically is enough to keep it looking new.
Can this be used as a security door?
Yes, it adds a significant layer of security. While primarily an insect screen, the robust build makes forced entry difficult. We recommend adding multi-point locks for maximum security when used on main entrances or balconies.
