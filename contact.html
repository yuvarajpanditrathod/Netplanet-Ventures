Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

Do I need an appointment to visit the showroom?

Walk-ins are welcome during business hours, but we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure dedicated consultation time with our experts.

How long does it take to get a quote?

For standard inquiries, we provide detailed quotes within 24 hours. Complex projects requiring site measurements may take 2-3 business days.

Do you provide free site measurements?

Yes, we offer complimentary site measurements for projects within our service area. Contact us to schedule a convenient time.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Hubballi, Dharwad, and surrounding areas. For locations outside this region, please contact us to discuss service availability.

Can I see product samples before ordering?

Absolutely! Our showroom displays a wide range of products, materials, and finishes. You can also request samples to take home for consideration.
